Week 2 deliverable

Recover soil parameters from a handful of readings

Given only a small set of measured (scaled distance, velocity) pairs, fit the attenuation exponent n and coupling constant k by Adam gradient descent in log space — the inverse of the forward model above.

Model being fit — log-space linear regression on two soil parameters
ln(V) = ln(k) − n · ln(Z)  ·  fit by Adam, 300 epochs, lr = 0.01
